How to install

1

Cut your strips

Cut BlueTex to your door width. The diagram above shows how many strips per door.

2

Apply tape at each seam

Run double-sided tape horizontally across the door at each panel seam — ~18" pieces, full width. The dashed lines in your diagram show where.

3

Press strips in from the bottom up

Start at the floor. Press each strip firmly onto the tape. Work upward. Keep them snug — no gaps.

4

Seal the seams

Run white seam tape over every horizontal joint. Press firmly edge to edge.
